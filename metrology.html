Metrology

3L Solutions supplies custom gages manufactured according to our customers' drawings. We offer various types of gages, such as spline gages, plug gages, ring gages, insert gages, plate gages, inspection arbors, and functional gages.

  • We specialize in gages with multiple critical dimensions
  • We meet exacting tolerances, up to +/- 0.002mm (0.00008")
  • Materials include various steel and carbide grades
  • Quick lead-times
  • Gages can be supplied with critical dimensions calibrated and certified to ISO 17025 by an accredited laboratory
