About Us

3L Solutions began in 2007 when a Ph.D. metallurgist identified the need for an alternative source of high precision tooling in the powdered metal industry. Upon our founding, we demonstrated our ability to provide cost-effective solutions for quality tooling and quickly developed a customer base. We started and continue to thrive in Northeast Ohio.

Our reliable tooling quality and lead-times, along with our commitment to service, earned us a superb reputation in the industry. As our customers began to request help on other products and services, we expanded to include additional product lines. Beyond high precision tooling and custom gages, we supply metal powders, low- to high-volume custom manufactured parts, and furnace-related products. Our established customer base includes companies of different sizes from around the globe, ranging from start-ups to Fortune 100 corporations.

We are committed to serving our customers and strive for total customer satisfaction.
